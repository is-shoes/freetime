Meet the Team!

In a quiet corner of Santa Barbara, overshadowed by poverty, Team freetime™, a group of determined youths, set out on a challenging journey. This team, comprising Owen, a skilled coder with a second-hand, cracked-screen laptop; Cyrus, a talented designer who sacrificed meals for internet access; Gayatri, a developer who studied by candlelight due to lack of electricity at home; and Angie, a hardworking single mom of three and natural leader, aspired to achieve something extraordinary:

WINNING THE SBHACK'S BEGINNER HACK AWARD WITH THEIR INNOVATIVE WEBSITE.

However, their path was littered with obstacles that seemed insurmountable. Poverty and starvation were their constant companions, and resources like stable internet, modern computers, or even a quiet place to work were luxuries they could hardly afford. Faced with hardships like poverty and limited resources, Team freetime™ showed remarkable resilience. They huddled together at Corwin Pavillion for its free Wi-Fi, sharing both their solitary laptop and humble meals of bread and water, yet remained fiercely dedicated to their goal.

Their project, an event-planning website named freetime™, aimed to help students find common free time for meetings. Cyrus's clean, efficient code, combined with Owen's determined back-end development, and Gayatri's intuitive, user-friendly designs, under Angie's diligent coordination, brought their vision to life.

Despite challenges like unreliable internet at UCSB and technical issues with their only laptop, the team persevered. Their successful creation of FreeTime™ is more than just a website: it symbolizes their victory over life's adversities and is a source of inspiration for many.
